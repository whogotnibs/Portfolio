Polar

2017
Educational Video Game
(Unity, Blender, Photoshop)

Partners: Laura Hirsh, Alex Kozina, Mirlande Germain
My Roles: concept, level design, pixel art

For my final-year Game Studio class, I was paired up with the non-profit organization LEARN Quebec to make an educational game. The game is intended to be complimentary to their curriculum and support a classroom discussion around the issue of climate change. Polar teaches students about humanityâ€™s impact on the environment, and has them consider futures both with and without change.

Eaches level teaches players about a new issue related to climate change, and has them navigate a polar bear through the Arctic terrain and experience the effects first-hand. As the game progresses the gameplay gets more challenging as sea ice starts to melt, shipping routes are created, and the bear gets forced further from its natural habitat.
