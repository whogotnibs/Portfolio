Web Flower (webpage)

2017
Web Game / App
(JavaScript, HTML, CSS)

The purpose of Web Flower is to push the boundaries of what people will make time for in their daily routines. Upon visiting the site, the responsibility of regularily watering a virtual flower is imposed onto users. Once the flower dies it will remain dead on the webpage forever, and it is up to the user to decide how much they value this virtual thing.

To simulate life, the program uses a health variable that goes down with time and up by ‘watering’ the plant. To exist in real time, the app calculates the amount of time that a user is away from the webpage, and the amount of health lost during that time. The health is stored in the device’s localStorage, which is what allows the app to remember the flower's status.
