About Me

I am a recent graduate of Concordia University in Montreal, where I recieved my BFA degree. The four-year Design program at Concordia focuses on exposing its students to a wide range of mediums, and has given me a strong base in the digital, tactile, and theoretical applications of design.

My interests range all the way from web programming to woodworking to illustration. My design aesthetic is heavily influenced by the internet and video games, and typically contains an element of interactivity.
