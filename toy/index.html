Toy Fingerboards (instagram)

2017 - 2018
Mini Skateboards
(Birch / Oak Veneer, Inkjet)

Partner: Ross LeBlanc
My Roles: manufacturing, graphics, marketing

Toy Fingerboards is an ongoing project that takes skateboard making, from the graphics to the woodworking, down to a smaller scale. These miniature skateboard decks can be set up and played with or simply put on display. Toy is currently run through our Instagram page, where we promote and sell our boards in small runs. Each deck is handmade from a selection of birch and oak veneer and pressed in our custom shaped mold. After that the decks are sanded down, holes are drilled, and the graphics are transferred.
