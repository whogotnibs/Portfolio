The Concordian Newspaper

2016 - 2017
Editorial Illustration
(Photoshop, Drawing Tablet)

This is a collection of editorial illustrations from three semesters of working at The Concordian Newspaper. The Concordian is a popular school newspaper, published both in print and online. As the Assistant Graphics Editor, I was responsible for illustrating 2-3 articles per week.
