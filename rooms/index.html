ROOMS (webpage)

2017
Web Game
(JavaScript, HTML, CSS)

ROOMS is a JavaScript-programmed game that I made for my Web Intervention class. The guidelines of this project were to create an interactive web experience that addresses how the advancement of digital technologies has inadvertently brought about new social issues. Through my project, I wanted to have the player consider how an interface has the ability to reveal and conceal the ideologies the designers behind it.

In the game, the player navigates in first person through a 3D maze. They collect items and notes in the form of image, audio, and text files that are downloaded onto their computer. The game's unfinished rooms, broken simulations, and generally bulky interface allow the player to peek behind the curtain of design, revealing the personality of its designer.
